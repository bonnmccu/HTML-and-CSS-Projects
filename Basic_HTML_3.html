Ode to the Fish

A Sonnet by - A Random Poem Generator

My pretty fish, you inspire me to write. I love the way you frighten, swim and glide, Invading my mind day and through the night, Always dreaming about the quiet tide. How do I love you? Let me count the ways. I love your vernal colors, eyes and fins. Thinking of your glossy eyes fills my days. My love for you is the accusing waves. Now I must away with a nitty heart. Remember my through words whilst we're apart.
